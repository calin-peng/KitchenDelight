English Muffin Sandwich

Ingredients
English Muffin
Ground Pork/Chicken
Corn starch(optional), Black Pepper, Salt, Curry Powder
Egg
Sliced Cheese
Butter
Preparation
Crack the egg and beat, leave one egg white out for the next stepfor traditional muffin sandwich we use whole egg, we beat it this time to let it better stand up to freezing and re-heating
Marinate the pork/chicken with egg white, starch, salt, pepper and curry powder
Steps
Spray some oil into the pan, pour in beaten egg once heated.I use pan with circular shape compartments. Egg ring works as well.
Let sit until cooked, flip and cook for another few second(light yellow is fine, don't char it)
Move the egg to a plate. Add oil(perferably non-spray)
Scoop ground meat, put into the compartment, press and shape it with the spoon so it fits in the circle
for egg ring, shape before putting into the pan
Cook over medium heat until golden brown. Flip and cook until golden brown.
Move to a plate.
Assembling
Spread butter onto the muffin , on the side that will be in contact with the ingredients
This acts as a waterproof layer to keep the muffin dry and fresh, you can put butter into microwave oven for about 10 seconds and stir it to make it easy to spread out.
Layer the egg, sliced cheese and meat patty in perferred order
Wrap with parchment paper and put into the freezing layer of the fridge
Before Serving
Move to the lower layer of the fridge 12h ahead(optional)
Heat in microwave oven for 2 minutes
Cook in air fryer(380F) for about 2 minutes
Enjoy!
