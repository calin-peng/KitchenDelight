DongPo Pork/东坡肉

Ingredients Pork Belly
Ginger, Green onion, Garlic
Crystal sugar, cooking wine, soy sauce
Preparation
Dice the pork belly
Cut the green onion into segments
Slice the ginger and Garlic
Steps
Blanch the pork with ginger an green onion until soft ( you should be able to pierce the skin with chopstick or fork easily by then)
Stir fry garlic, green onion , cooking wine, crystal sugar and soy sauce
Turn to high heat, braise the pork until coloured
Transfer to the clay pot, simmer with lid on
Remove the solid seasonings (i.e. ginger, etc.)
Fry until 1/2 of the liquid is evaporated
Remove the pork
Thicken the sauce ( the remaining in the clay) in the regular pan using starch water, pour it onto the pork
Enjoy!
