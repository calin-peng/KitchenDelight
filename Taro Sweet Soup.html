Taro Sweet Soup

Ingredients

Taro, Taro ball(optional)
Coconut milk, Milk, Sugar

Preparation

Bring a pot of water to boil, add taro ball, cook until medium high heat, stir occasionally to prevent sticking. After the balls float onto the water surface, turn to low heat, cook until preferred doneness, transfer the balls into icy water to obtain bouncy texture
Dice the taro

Steps

Add cold water into pot, to a point where taro dices can be precisely immersed
Cook taro in cold water until well done
Stir with spoon, use the spoon bottom to press taro dices. This step is to smash some of the taro, giving the soup a condense texture
Add coconut milk, stir well
Add milk according to personal perference, stir well
Add sugar, stir well
Cook in low heat, be mindful that not to boil the liquid, or the diary products might layer out
Add taro ball, stir well and cook for approx. 1 min
Enjoy!
