Omelette Rice Bento

Ingredients
Rice,
Ketchup, luncheon meat, corn
Egg Preparation
Cook the rice
Dice the luncheon meat Steps
Stir-fry the rice with luncheon meat and corns, add ketchup and coat the rice, stir fry for another 1 min,
add salt/pepper to flavor if needed
Make an egg crepe To Assemble
Lay the crepe on a piece of plastic wrap
Put the rice on it, leave some margin
Wrap the rice with teh crepe
Place a bowl/plate upside down, covering the rice and crepe, hold the content in place by hand then flip the bowl
Enjoy!
