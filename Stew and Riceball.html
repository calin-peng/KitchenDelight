Stew and Riceball

Ingredients
Carrot, White Onion, Tofu Skin, Konjac
Kombu, Dried Shiitake, Miso Paste
Mirin, Light Soy Sauce, sugar Preparation
Stew
Slice the ginger( 4-5 slices)
Dice the carrot
Strip the onion
Japanese style stock
Soak Kombu and shiitake in warm water until they become soft and expanded
With the water you used to soak them, simmer in the saucepan until it starts to bubble
Don't simmer until fully boiled otherwise the kombu will seep mucus out
Remove kombu and shiitake, save the stock for later
Rice ball seasoning
Dice the shiitake ( cut 6-9 pieces out of each) and cut kombu in small pieces, mix with miso paste(1/2 tbsp)
Cook the rice (ideally sushi rice) Steps
Stew
Add oil and ginger to the pan, fry until aromatic
Add carrot and onion, stir fry until aromatic
Remove ginger from the pot, add Konjac, fry until the water in it got extracted
Add the stock (and some water if needed)
Add light soy sauce ,sugar and mirin, simmer over medium heat without lid until the carrot is softened
Turn to low heat, add tofu skin, cook to perferred texture Riceball
Stir-fry shiitake and kombu with soy sauce
Mix it into rice and made it into riceball
Serve with the stew. Enjoy!
