Tonkotsu Ramen

Ingredients
Sliced Pork Belly
Green onion, Nori Seaweed
Soy sauce, Sugar, Miso Paste, Mirin, Milk, chicken stock Preparation
Shred the green top of the green onion
Cut the Nori into pieces Steps
>Fry the pork belly with soy sauce, mirin and sugar until golden brown and crispy, remove from the pan
Mix chicken stock, miso, mirin, soy sauce, sugar and milk in the pan, heat over low heat
Do not bring it to boil or the milk may separate
Cook the ramen noodle, serve with pork, soup and nori. Disperse the green onion on top. Enjoy!
