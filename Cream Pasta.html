Cream Pasta

Ingredients
Mushroom cream, Milk, Butter
Bacon, Onion
Cheddar Cheese, Black pepper, Parsley Preparation
Cut the bacon into pieces
Do a cross section of the onion and leave them in strips
Mince the garlic Steps
Add butter to the pan, when melted, stir-fry with onion & garlic until aromatic
Add bacon, stir-fry until golden brow
Add water and milk at 1:1 by volume, cover the ingredients by 1-2 cm
Add Spaghetti , make sure it is covered in liquid
If not, top the liquid off by milk
Simmer without lid, stir occasionally to prevent the pasta from sticking to each other
When the water bubbles, add mushroom cream, stir until it evenly integrates into the liquid
Cook with lid on until the pasta is well done
Add cheese, stir well
you can use slurry to add the the density of the topping
Add black pepper, parsley, etc.
Enjoy!
