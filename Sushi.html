Sushi

Ingredients
Nori seaweed, Rice(ideally short grain)
Sausage (or any meat you like), Egg,
Carrot, Cucumber(or any vegetable)
The options for the filling can be very vast and flexible
Salt
Preparation
Cook the rice You don't need a rice cooker. If you are using saucepan, remember to add approx. 2 times as you need with rice cooker( approx. 1.5 cm over the rice)
Add salt to the rice, mix well, then put the lid on to maintain moisture
Strip the carrot and cucumber, add salt and mix
we want them to sweat
Beat 1-2 eggs
Cut the sausage into strips
Steps
Add oil to the pan, pour the egg in to make egg crepe, then remove and cut into strips
Sauté the sausage, remove;
Sauté the carrot & cucumber strips Assemble
spread the rice on the nori(cover approx. 60 % of its surface),press well
In that order, place egg strips, sausage and veggie strips on the middle of the rice
Roll the nori until the two lengths of the "rice rectangle" meet, press well
You may want to hold the filling with your finger to make sure they stay in place
Continue rolling until a cylinder is shaped
Hold it tight in your hand and clench to make it firm
Cut and enjoy!
