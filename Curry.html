Curry

Ingredients
Milk, Curry Cubes , and
anything you have no idea to deal with in your fridge!
Today I'll use :
Potato, Long Bean, Chicken Thigh and Pepper
Preparation

Peel and dice the potato
Dice the chicken
Cut the long beans into segments
Steps
Stir-fry the chicken with pepper until well-done
Add potato, stir-fry until aromatic
Pour in cold water and milk at approx. 1:1, immerse the ingredients
Simmer until boiled, when the potato is almost ready, add long beans and curry slice,
stir well until curry dissolve
You can adjust the flavour by cheese, pepper. etc according to your taste
You can also add starch to thicken the soup
Cook until perferred doneness
Enjoy!
