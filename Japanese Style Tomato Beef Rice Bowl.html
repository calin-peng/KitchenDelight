Japanese Style Tomato Beef Rice Bowl

Ingredients
Sliced Beef
Tomato
Soy Sauce, Mirin, Sugar
Preparation
Peel the tomato by cutting a cross on it and soaking in boiled water for about 5min
Dice the tomato
Marinate the beef with soy sauce, mirin and sugar (2:1:0.5)
Steps
Add oil to the pan, fry the beef slices, flip when one side is golden brown and continue with the other side
When the beef is fully cooked, add the tomato, stir fry at low-medium heat, break the lumps of tomato with spatula
Cook until perferred doneness, serve with rice
Enjoy!
