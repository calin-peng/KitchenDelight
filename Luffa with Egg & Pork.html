Luffa with Egg & Pork

Ingredients
Luffa, egg
Pork
Soy sauce, sugar, pepper, starch
Garlic
Preparation
Chop the luffa into slices
Crack the egg & blend well
Strip the pork, marinate with soy sauce
Mince the garlic
Make starch water with 1 tbsp of starch Steps
Stir-fry the egg, remove for future use
Stir-fry the garlic until aromatic
Add the pork , stir fry until done
Add the luffa, stir fry until aromatic
Add water, immerse all the ingredients in the pan
Simmer with lid on until the luffa is done
Add soy sauce, sugar and pepper according to your taste
Add starch water , stir until the liquid condensed
Add scrambled egg, stir fry for seconds
Serve in the plate and enjoy!
