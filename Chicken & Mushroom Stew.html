Chicken & Mushroom Stew

Ingredients
Chicken Tights, Shiitake Mushroom, Green Onion, Ginger
Oyster Sauce, Soy Sauce
Starch, White Pepper
Preparation
Dice the chicken and mushroom
Chop the green onion stalk into 3 sticks, and the green tops into pieces(for further use)
Slice the ginger(3 slices)
Marinate the chicken with green onion stalk, ginger slices, oyster sauce, soy sauce , white pepper and mushroom
Ratio b/n sauces oyster : soy = 1:3 approx. by volume
After about 30 minutes, remove the green onion and ginger, add starch(1-2 tbsp) and oil (approx.1 tbsp), mix well until the solid is evenly coated
Steps
Steam for about 20 minutes, stir and flip in the middle for even heat
Sprinkle the chopped onion greens on the top, enjoy!
