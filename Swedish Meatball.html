Swedish Meatball

Ingredients
For Mashed Potato
Potato, Heavy Cream, butter, black pepper, salt For Meatball
Ground Beef, Ground Pork
Egg, Bread crumb, White Onion, Salt, Black Pepper, White Pepper, milk For Sauce
Butter, flour, beef stock, vegetable stock, Heavy Cream,
Worcester sauce, Dijon Mustard, Soy Sauce
Preparation
For Mashed Potato
Peel and roll-cut the potato For Meatball
Mince the onion
Mix the beef and pork
Steps
For Mashed Potato
Put potato in room temperature water, add salt, bring to boil,
cook over low/medium heat until softened, then move into a bowl
Heat heavy cream with butter
Mash the potato to 50% mashed
Add cream&butter mix , then smash
Continue to add the butter mix for about 2-3 more times until the potato is fully mashed Keep some clumps if you want
Add salt and pepper For Meatball
Mix the ground beef and pork, marinate with :
Minced Onions, bread crumb, egg, milk, salt, black pepper, white pepper
Roll the mixture into meatballs
Saut√© with oil in the frying pan
To finish up, cook in the oven/ air fryer to 1-2 minutes For Sauce
Use the pan that was used for frying meatball, add butter, when melted, add flour
mix until well-combined and there's no clump
Add beef stock, mix well
Add vegetable stock, mix well
Add heavy cream, mix well
Add Worcester sauce, Dijon mustard and soy sauce
Can add salt for heavier flavour
Add the meatball back in , stir with the sauce for another one minute
Enjoy!
