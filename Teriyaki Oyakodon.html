Teriyaki Oyakodon

Ingredients

Chicken Tight, White Onion,Eggs
Teriyaki Sauce, Soy Sauce, Chicken Broth, Cooking Wine

Preparation

Remove the bone in chicken tight
Marinate the chicken tight with Teriyaki Sauce and Soy sauce( 2.5:1 by volume)
To make the chicken tender you can also add some honey
Strip the white onion
Crack the eggs and stir until blended

Steps

Fry the chicken with the skin facedown until the skin turns golden brown
Turn to low heat, cook with lid on until done
Remove the chicken tights
Stir fry the onion until gold, simmer in chicken broth until soft and translucent
Cut the chicken tight to preferred size, put them into the pan
Add cooking wine, simmer with lid on until aromatic
Pour half of the egg liquid into the pan, simmer with lid on until egg is done
Pour in the other half , cook to preferred doneness
Enjoy!
