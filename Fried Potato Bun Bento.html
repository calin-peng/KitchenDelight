Fried Potato Bun Bento

Ingredients
White Onion
Ground beef
Soy Sauce, Sugar
Egg, Starch, Panko
Potato, Milk, Pepper
Preparation
Mince the white onion
Beat the Egg
Dice the potato and boil, mash and add milk for smooth texture, add pepper for flavour
Steps
Stir-fry the beef and white onion until aromatic
Add soy sauce and sugar, stir-fry until well done
Ideally the beef will be golden brown (partially charred) and the onion will be translucent
Mix with mashed potato and starch (so everything is held in tightly)
Coat with starch , then egg, then panko
Deep-fry until golden brown
Enjoy!
