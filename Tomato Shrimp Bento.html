Tomato Shrimp Bento

Ingredients
Shrimp, Corn Startch
Soy sauce, ketchup, soy bean paste, mirin, white pepper(1:4:1:1:0.5)
Preparation
Mix the soy sauce, ketchup, soy bean paste, mirin and white pepper
Coat the srimp with corn starch
Steps
Pan-fry the shrimp until golden brown
Add the sauce, mix well and stir fry until fully cooked
Serve with rice. Enjoy!
