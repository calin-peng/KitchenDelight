Clay Pot Rice

Ingredients
Sausage, Chicken thigh, Egg, Scallion,
Oil, Ric
Soy sauce, Oyster sauce, White Pepper,
Preparation
Soak the rice in the warm water for approx.20 min, then drain
Dice the chicken thigh, marinate with soy sauce, oyster sauce and white peppe
Cut the scallion up
Steps
Brush oil over the interior of the clay pot
to form crust in the rice
Put the rice in, add cold water that covers it
Cook with lid on, after the water is significantly heated up ,add chicken thigh If there's marination juice left over, add it
Cook with lid on, after the water boils, add sausage
You can cut it into segments for faster cooking
Cook for a while with lid on
Enjoy!
