Japenese Style Beef and Potato Stew

Ingredients

White Onion, Sliced Beef, Potato, Soy Sauce

Preparation

Strip the onion and dice the potato

Steps

Stir-fry the sliced beef until well done.
Comparing to stew the beef directly, this step can help drive the fishery smell away,and blend some distinctive flavour into the soup.
Add stripped onion when the beef is half-done, fry until translucent.
Add cold water, immerse the ingredients.
Add soy sauce, boil until the potato is soft that can be easily pierced by fork or chopstick.
Enjoy!
