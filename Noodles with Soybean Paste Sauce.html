Noodle with Soybean Paste Sauce

Ingredients
Ground Pork
Soy bean sauce, Sweet bean sauce, Sugar
Garlic, Scallion
Cucumber
Noodle
Corn starch Preparation
Mix the soy bean sauce, sweet bean sauce and soy sauce, save as Bowl A
Mince the garlic and scallion, save as Bowl B
Strip the cucumber
Cook the noodle, soak in cold(room temperature) water
Way to cook noodle may vary depends on the noodle of your choice
Prepare one bowl of starch water ( starch:water by volume: >= 1:1) Steps
Add oil to the wok, add ground pork
Sauté until golden brown, flip and sauté until golden brown
Split the pork using spatula, add the content of Bowl B , stir fry until aromatic
Add the content of Bowl A, mix well, stir fry until aromatic
Add water, simmer under medium-high heat with lid off
Test the degree of sweetness, add sugar based on your liking
Drain the noodle from the water, mix well with sauce and cucumber strip
Enjoy!
