Soy Sauce Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients
Drumsticks
Light Soy Sauce, Dark Soy Sauce, Cooking Wine, Crystal Sugar, White Pepper
Ginger, Garlic
Preparation
Mix the Light Soy Sauce ,Dark Soy Sauce, Cooking Wine and white pepper(3:3:2:0.5 tbsp)
Slice the ginger, peel the garlic
Pierce the chicken leg on its thickest parts
Steps
Blanch the drumssticks
This step is to get rid of the fat in the skin
Pan-fry the ginger and garlic until aromatic, then add 1/2 cup of water to help it cook
After the water is boiled, add 2/3 of the sauce
Put the chicken thigh in, make sure 2/3 of it is covered in liquid
Pour the rest of the sauce on the top of chicken to help it get coloured
Cook with lid on at low heat, flip occasionally until all sauce is reduced down
Enjoy!
