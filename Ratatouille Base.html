Ratatouille (Base)

Ingredients
White Onion, Carrot, Celery, Canned Diced Tomato, Bell Pepper(ideally yellow and red)
Garlic, Butter
Salt, Sugar, Black Pepper
Cheddar Cheese
Thyme, Basil Preparation
Burn the bell pepper in the air fryer until they are charred on the surface(approx. 15 min), then peel and dice
Dice the onion , carrot and celery
Stir-fry the onion and carrot in the pot until aromatic
Add the canned tomato, remember to drain the liquid if there is any in the can
Add the diced bell pepper
Add the celery, mix well, let sit until aromatic
Shred the garlic(2-3 cloves), add in
Add the butter
Mix well , cook with lid on for approx. 40 min (until all the ingredients are soft)
Add salt , black pepper , thyme and basil, mix well
Remove the lid, cook for about another 25 min , add cheese according to personal perference
Mash it in the blender My plan was to make Ratatouille but I failed at the last baking step, the vegetable sliced seemed undercooked, I believed that that was because I was using air fryer which does not have a slow-baking function as the oven does.
However, this sauce base is perfect! It comes very well with the pasta.
I may come back to it at some point in the future to complete my ratatouille project.
