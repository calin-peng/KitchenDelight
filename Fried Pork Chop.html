Fried Pork Chop

Ingredients
Pork Chop
Ground Beef
Salt, Pepper
Panko , Egg, starch, flour
Chicken Stock
Preparation
Cut the white tendon on the pork chops into segments
So the pork won't curl up when you fry it
Marinate the pork with salt and pepper
Mix the starch and flour at 1:3 by volume
Crack and whisk the eggs
Coat the pork chops with the mix of starch&flour
Dip it in the egg liquid
Coat the chops with panko
Steps
>

Deep-fry the pork chops , then remove
Pour the oil in the pan out, pour in the Chicken stock
To a point of depth where the pork can half-immerse in the stock
Put the chops back into the pan, cook until boil
Pour in the rest of egg liquid until the liquid solidify
Enjoy!
