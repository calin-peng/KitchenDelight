Braised Chicken w/Potato

Ingredients
Chicken Thigh, Potato
Ginger, Scallion
Soy Bean Sauce, Light Soy Sauce, Dark Soy Sauce, Sweet Bean Paste, Cooking Wine Preparation
Slice the Ginger
Cut the Scallion green
Roll-cut the potato and chicken thigh To make Sauce
Mix soy bean sauce , light soy sauce, dark soy sauce , sweet bean paste and cooking wine
(1 : 3 : 1 : 1 : 3) Steps
Stir-fry the potato until slightly caramelized, remove and save
Stir-fry chicken thigh with ginger until aromatic, remove the ginger
Add the potato, stir- fry until aromatic
Add the Sauce, stir-fry until it evenly coat on the ingredients
Add water approx. 0.5 cm over the ingredients, simmer over medium heat without lid until the potato softens and the sauce cling to the ingredients
Sprinkle the scallion green. Enjoy!
