Omelet Rice with Seafood Tomato Sauce

Ingredients
Tomato, Carrot, Onion, Egg
Shrimp
Ketchup, Salt, Pepper
Preparation
Dice the tomato, carrot and onion
Crack and whisk the egg
Cut the shrimps into piecies
Recommendation : 3 segments each
Cooking
Omelet Egg
Put butter into the frying pan, add the egg when the butter melts
Using a spoon, stir slowly until the egg starts to solidify
The spoon'd better be wooden or silica
Before the egg solidifies completely, remove the omelet egg from the pan
Seafood Tomato Sauce
Stir-fry the shrimp until well done
Add the carrot and onion,
Add some salt and pepper, stir fry until aromatic
Turn to low heat, cook with lid on until the carrots starts to soften
You can add water if needed
Turn to medium heat
Add tomato, stir fry until aromatic & liquid oozes out
Brew with lid on, until the carrot softened and is ready to be eaten
Serve with omelet egg and rice. Enjoy!
