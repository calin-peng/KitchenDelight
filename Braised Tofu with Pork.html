Braised Tofu with Pork

Ingredients
Ground Pork, Garlic , Green Onion, Firm Tofu
Cooking Wine, Soy Sauce
Preparation
Marinate ground pork with minced garlic ,minced green onion, soy sauce and cooking wine.
(soy sauce : cooking wine = 3:1 by volume)
Dice the tofu, stir-fry until golden brown. Remove.
Steps
Fry the ground pork , using the same way as frying a steak - don't flip until one side is golden brown
(by this way we can get rid of the fishery smell in the pork) Add tofu and cold water(immerse the ingredients)
Add soy sauce.
Stew with lid on. Add oyster sauce and braised sauce if needed.
Wait until boiling.
Crack two eggs, stir, no need to blend the white and yolk completely.
Use a cup with curve on the brim, pour half of the the egg into wok slowly and evenly.
Put the lid on, wait until the egg is well done.
Pour in the other half of the egg, boil with lid on until perfered doneness.
Enjoy!
