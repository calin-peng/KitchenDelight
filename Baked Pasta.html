Baked Pasta

Ingredients

Onion, Garlic
Ground Beef
Oil, Salt, Black Pepper, red wine, sugar
Canned Smashed Tomato
Shredded Cheese
Spaghetti
Preparation
Dice the Onion and Garlic
Steps
Pour oil into the frying pan
Add garlic, stir-fry until aromatic
Add onion, stir-fry until aromatic
Add the ground beef, spread salt and black pepper on it, don't stir until one side is golden brown
Flip the ground beef, fry until golden brown
This step is to Stir-fry all the ingredients until aromatic
Add red wine
Add the smashed tomato,
Add salt, sugar and black pepper
Cook for appprox. 2 min
Remove from the pan
Cook the spaghetti to approx. 75% done
Mix the spaghetti and sauce , spread shredded cheese
You can add one more layer of sauce and cover it by cheese if you want
Pre-heat the air fryer to 400 F
Put the pasta into the air fryer, cook until the cheese melts
Enjoy!
