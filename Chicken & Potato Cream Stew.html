Chicken & Potato Cream Stew

Ingredients
Chicken Thigh
Potato, White Onion, White Mushroom, garlic
Milk, Cheese Shred, Flour
Black pepper, Salt, Parsley
Preparation
Roll-Cut the Potato and mushroom
Dice the onion
Mince the garlic
Steps
Add oil to the pan, fry chicken thigh in it with the skin down, no need to flip, remove after the skin is caramelized and crispy
Add oil to the pan, fry potatoes in it until caramelized
Add mushroom, stir-fry until aromatic, remove potato and mushroom
Add Butter to the pan, stir-fry garlic and onion until aromatic
Add oil to the pan and add the flour
Add incrementally if you are unsure about the dosage, flour is good at thickening the liquid
Stir fry until the flour turns translucent and evenly coat on the onion
Add milk and mix well
You can add milk any time during the following process
Cut the chicken thigh based on your preference, add it in
Add the potato and mushroom, simmer over medium heat until the potato is softened
Add salt, cheese shred and pepper, mix well
Serve in plate, sprinkle the parsley flakes
Enhoy!
