Thousand-leaf Tofu

Ingredients
Thousand-leaf Tofu, Green Onion, Ginger
Pork Belly Soy sauce, Yellow bean sauce, cooking wine
Preparation
Slice the tofu and the pork belly.
Soak tofu in cold or warm water to soften it.
Slice the ginger.
Cut the green onion into segments
Steps
Fry the tofu slices with oil or blanch them.Remove.
Frying with oil can make it more aromatic and can gives better texture, but simply blanching is also good(I tries in both ways and there's no huge difference)
Add some oil, stir-fry the ginger until aromatic.
Add the pork, yellow bean sauce and soy sauce at 2.5:1 by volume. Fry until aromatic.
Soy sauce is for colouring.
You can remove the ginger then if you don't like them to be in the dish.
Add tofu slices and green onion. Add cooking wine ( no more than 1/2 tbsp).
Fry until aromatic.
Enjoy!
