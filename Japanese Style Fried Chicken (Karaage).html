Japanese Style Fried Chicken (Karaage)

Ingredients
Chicken thigh
Egg, soy sauce ,mirin ,salt, white pepper Preparation
Dice the chicken thighs, marinate with mirin, soy sauce, salt and white pepper
Crack an egg into the chicken, blend well
Add one tbsp of water into starch, stir. We want it to coagulate!
Coat the chicken with starch Steps
Deep fry the chicken until light golden brown, remove
Leave the oil to raise its temperature until bubbling
Put the chicken back there, fry until golden brown
Enjoy!
