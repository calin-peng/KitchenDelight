Taiwanese Style Braised Pork/台式卤肉

Ingredients
Purple Onion, Pork Belly, Crystal Sugar, Soy Sauce,Oyster Sauce, Quail Eggs(optional)
Preparation
Cut the pork belly into cuboids
Strip the onion
Steps
Fry the purple onion until they are crispy. Remove.
Fry the pork belly until the oil inside start to ooze.
Add crystal sugar, crack them up with spatula.
Add soy sauce to colour the pork. ( A little a time , it is effective in colouring!)
Fry the pork until well done.
Add cold water, immerse all the ingredients.
Add Soy sauce and Oyster sauce at the volume ratio 2.5:1 approximately.
Stew with lid on, for at least 40 minutes in total.
Give it a taste , add flavour if needed.
Add quail eggs, continue stewing for a while.
Enjoy!
