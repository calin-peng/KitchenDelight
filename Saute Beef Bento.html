Saute Beef Bento

Ingredients
Beef
Green Onion
Apple
Salt, Soy Sauce, Mirin
Mixed vegetables (I used Spinach)
Egg
Preparation
Slice the Beef
Marinate overnight with green onion ,diced apple and a bit soy sauce & a pinch of salt
Make Rice and set in the fridge overnight
Steps
Beef
Saute the beef until well done, remove from the pan
Mix soy sauce and mirin in the pan, at 1:0.8
Add the beef back to the pan, stir-fry and mix well
Vegetables
Boil water in the saucepan, add a pinch of salt
Blanch the vegetable for about 5 min
Remove and drain the water
Rice
Make scramble egg, remove
Add rice, stir-fry with scrambled egg

Assemble the three courses to make bento. enjoy!
