Spaghetti Bolongnese

Ingredients
Diced Tomato, Grounded beef , Celery, White Onion, Carrot
Pepper, Ketchup
Preparation
Mince the Celery, White Onion and Carrot in the food chopper
Use of machine is not necessary, just for the sake of saving time:(
Don't mince separately if u r using machine, we want them to mix
Steps
Pour oil into the saucepan, fry the minced veggies until fragrant
There may me some liquid ooze, you can absorb the excessive with kitchen towel or wait until they evaporate
Put the minced beef in, stir-fry until fragrant well cooked
Pour the diced tomatoes in
Simmer for approx. 20 min, then add the ketchup
Cook until perferred doneness
Enjoy!
