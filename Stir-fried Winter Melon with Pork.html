Stir-fried Winter Melon & Pork

Ingredients Winter Melon, Ground Pork
Oyster Sauce, Soy Sauce
Salt, Sugar, Chicken Powder(optional) Preparation Slice the winter melon Steps Fry the ground pork , using the same way as frying a steak - don't flip until one side is golden brown
( by this way we can get rid of the fishery smell in the pork)
Put the slice winter melon in.
Stir the melon up for few times, then add cold water, half-immerse the ingredients.
Add oyster sauce and soy sauce, at the ratio of 3:1 approximately.(by the volume)
You can add some chicken powder to make it more delicacy.
Stir so the sauce dissolve evenly in the water.
With the lid on, bring it to boil.
Remove the lid, boil until the winter melon is soft and translucent.
Enjoy!
