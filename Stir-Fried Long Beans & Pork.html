Japenese Style Beef and Potato Stew

Ingredients

Long beans, ground pork, garlic, green onion
Soy sauce, cooking wine, chicken broth

Preparation

Cut the long beans into small segments.
Marinate ground pork with minced garlic ,minced green onion, soy sauce and cooking wine.
(soy sauce : cooking wine = 3:1 by volume)

Steps

Blanch the long beans. Remove.
Fry the ground pork , using the sae way as frying a steak - don't flip until one side is golden brown
( by this way we can get rid of the fishery smell in the pork)
Add the lone beans and a few chicken broth, stir fry until aromatic.
Boil with lid on until longs beans are soft.
Enjoy!
