Beef & Potato Cream Stew

Ingredients

Potato, White Onion,
Beef Rib Finger
Chicken or Beef Broth
Milk
Pepper
Preparation
Cut the Beef Rib into pieces,
Boil until done, remove excessive water
Dice the potato,
Cut the onion into strips
Steps
Stir-fry onion until translucent
Add potato, stir-fry to a preferred point
Add beef, stir fry until aromatic
Add the broth and milk at approx. 4:1 by volume
Add pepper
Bring to boil
Turn to low heat, simmer with lid on until the ingredients are soft and penetrateable
Enjoy!
