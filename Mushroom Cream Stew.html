Mushroom Cream Stew

Ingredients
Brocoli , Carrot, Potato
Chicken Thigh
Condensed Mushroom Cream Soup
Pepper
Sliced Cheese Preparation
Dice the Vegetables
Dice the Chicken Thigh Steps
Stir-fry chicken thigh until well done
Add the potato and carrot, stir-fry until aromatic
Add water, immerse the ingredients, simmer with lid on
When the water is warm, add the mushroom soup, stir until evenly dissolved
Simmer with lid on
After the water boils, add the brocoli
Simmer until all ingredients are soft
Add sliced cheese, stir until dissolved
Sparkle some pepper, enjoy!
