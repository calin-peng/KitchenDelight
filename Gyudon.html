Gyudon(Japanese Beef Rice Bowl)

Ingredients
White Onion, Sliced Beef
Mirin, Soy Sauce, Sugar
Egg
Preparation
Cut the onion into thin slices (cross the fibre), then break into strips by hand
Steps
Prepare water in a pot, add mirin, soy sauce and sugar (1:2:0.5)
Add the beef, simmer at high heat with lid on
When it starts to bubble, add onion, simmer at medium heat with lid on
Check occasionally. When the onion becomes translucent, turn to high heat and cook with lid off until the juice is reduced down
Crack an egg into the pan(optional), cook with lid on until perferred doneness
Serve with rice. Enjoy!
