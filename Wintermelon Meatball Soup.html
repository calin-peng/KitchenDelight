Wintermelon Meatball Soup

Ingredients
Ground Pork
Soy sauce, Oyster Sauce, Salt
Ginger
Egg
Wintermelon
Preparation
Dice or slice the winter melon
Meatball
Marinate the ground pork with soy sauce, oyster sauce and salt(1:0.5:0.5)
Add one egg white into the mixture
Mince the ginger and mix into the meat
Roll the mixture into meatballs
Steps
Cook the winter melon in cold water until boiling
Add meatballs, cook at medium heat until the meatballs float to the water surface
Add salt based on personal perference
Enjoy!
