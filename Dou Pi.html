Dou Pi/Wuhan Pancake/豆皮

Ingredients
Egg, Dried Shiitake Mushroom, Dried Tofu, Ginger
Pork Belly
Glutinous Rice
Dark & Light soy sauce
Salt, Sugar
Preparation
1. Egg Wrap/omelette
Crack and beat the egg, add oil to the pan
Heat the oil, Pour the liquid egg in
Wait until it's hot enough to pour. Test : drip a bit egg liquid into the oil, if it bubbles with sizzling sound, the oil is considered to be hot enough
When the bottom of the omelette solidifies, flip and wait for few seconds, remove from the pan
Glutinous Rice Base
Rinse and put directly into the rice cooker, cook with the same way you cook the rice，the water should be about 0.5 cm deep over the rice
2.Topping
Soak the shiitake mushroom in water to rehydrate, you may want to use this water in following step
the higher the water temperature is, the faster the rehydrate will be
Dice dried tofu, soak in warm water with one tbsp salt
Dice the pork belly
Mince the ginger
Steps
Add oil to the wok/pan, stir fry pork belly and ginger until fragrant and golden brown
Add mushroom and dried tofu,
Add dark soy sauce, light soy sauce and sugar, stir fry until coloured and aromatic
Add water, cover the ingredients
Use the water you used to soak the dried shiitake to make it more flavourful
Cook for at least 30 minutes over medium heat. Liquid doesn't have to be drained Check occasionally, you may want to add flavour
Remove the topping from the pan, put the egg wrap in it
Put glutinous rice on the wrap, press to flatten it
Put the toppings on the rice, press
Put it into the plate, flip it
Cut it into pieces and enjoy!
