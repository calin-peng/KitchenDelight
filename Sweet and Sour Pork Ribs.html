Sweet and Sour Pork Ribs

Ingredients
Pork Ribs
Bell Pepper, Onion, Garlic, Ginger
Ketchup, Soy Sauce(light/dark), Chicken Stock, Sugar
Corn Starch(optional)
Preparation
Blanch the pork ribs, label as Bowl A
Dice the bell pepper and onion, label as Bowl B
Grate the garlic and ginger, label as Bowl C
Steps
Add oil and Bowl B to the wok, stir-fry for 1 min
Add Bowl A, stir-fry until golden brown (or at least aromatic!), remove from the wok and label as Bowl D
Add oil and Bowl C, stir fry until aromatic
Add ketchup and sugar, stir fry until aromatic
Add Bowl D, add chicken stock and water (approx.1:1), it should cover the ribs
Add light soy sauce
We want the pork rib to get coloured, so
Add proper amount of dark soy sauce based on your observation on the ribs
Stir well, simmer at medium heat with lid on for approx. 40 minutes, flip the pork ribs occasionally
Ideally you will see the liquid almost completely evaporate and it will stick on to the ribs as you pick them up
Otherwise you can simmer it for longer or to thicken it with starch water
Enjoy!
