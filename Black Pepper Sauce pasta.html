Black Pepper Sauce pasta

Ingredients
White Mushroom, White Onion, Egg
Pepper(Powder), soy sauce, starch
Pasta Preparation
Cook the pasta in boiling waterDoesn't have to be well-done at this point
Dice the mushroom and onion
Prepare a bowl of starch water Steps
Crack and egg into the frying pan, fry itI'm making it half-done to enjoy fluid yolk so I'm not going to flip it
Remove the egg, add onion and mushroom, stir-fry until aromatic
Pour water in, immense all ingredients
Cook with lid on
Add soy sauce and pepper, blend
Cook until aromatic At this point the mushroom and onion should be well-done, make sure you check the doneness.
Add starch water to thicken the sauce
Add pasta, stir
Move the pasta into he plate, put the egg on the top
I like to mix the egg with pasta when eating
Enjoy!
